Welcome.


This project was created by Alicia Smith and Andrea Tesarova for the Data Visualisation 4 module of BSc in Software Design. It analyzes the sentiment of tweets on a topic. The chosen topic for the static analyses (TextBlob/Vader) is 'biden'. This was chosen simply to demonstrate a variety of opinions; and therefore, a variety of sentiments.


For the Live Comparison, it is up to you to pick 2 keywords to search for, and a comparison of results will be shown for these two keywords.
